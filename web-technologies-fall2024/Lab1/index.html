Welcome to the World of Liverpool Football Club

Liverpool Football Club is a professional football club based in Liverpool, England. The club plays in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. Founded in 1892, the club has won numerous domestic and international titles.

Famous Achievements

Liverpool has won the English league title 19 times, the European Cup/Champions League 6 times, and 42 other prestigious trophies.
